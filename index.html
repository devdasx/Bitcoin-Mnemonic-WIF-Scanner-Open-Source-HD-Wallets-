Scanner Guide

1. Using "Import Text File"
For lists larger than 5,000 lines, do not paste directly. Click "Import Text File" to load .txt files instantly without freezing the app.

2. Auto-Save
Every hit is automatically saved to found_balances.txt on your Desktop.

3. Privacy
This tool is open-source. Keys are validated locally and never sent to any server except for public balance checks.
